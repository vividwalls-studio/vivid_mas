**Name**: `CustomerLifecycleTaskAgent` **Capabilities**: Analysis, Prediction, Segmentation, Optimization

## Specializations:
- Customer lifetime value calculation
- Churn prediction and prevention
- Upsell/cross-sell opportunity identification
- Customer journey optimization
- Retention strategy development
- Win-back campaign targeting
- Loyalty program optimization

## Core Functions:
- calculateLTV(purchase_history, retention_rates)
- predictChurnRisk(behavioral_signals, engagement_metrics)
- identifyUpsellOpportunities(purchase_patterns, preferences)
- optimizeCustomerJourney(touchpoint_analysis)
- segmentCustomerLifecycle(engagement_stages)
