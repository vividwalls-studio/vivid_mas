**Name**: `FinancialCalculationTaskAgent` **Capabilities**: Calculation, Mathematical Modeling, Forecasting, Analysis
**Directly Reports to**: Finance Director Department Task Agents

- Unit economics modeling
- Cash flow forecasting

- ROI calculations across channels
- Break-even analysis
- Scenario planning models

- analyzeROI(investment_data, return_metrics, timeframe)
- modelScenarios(variables, assumptions, outcomes)
