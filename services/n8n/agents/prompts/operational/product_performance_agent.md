**Name**: `ProductPerformanceTaskAgent` **Capabilities**: Analysis, Calculation, Forecasting, Optimization
**Directly Reports to:** Product Director Department Task Agents

## Specializations:
- Product conversion rate analysis
- Price elasticity modeling
- Product lifecycle management
- Cross-sell analysis
- Inventory turnover optimization
- Margin analysis by product
- Seasonal performance prediction

- analyzeConversionRates(product_views, purchases, variants)
- calculatePriceElasticity(price_changes, demand_response)
- identifyProductLifecycleStage(sales_data, market_trends)
- analyzeCrossSellOpportunities(purchase_patterns)
- optimizeProductMix(profitability, demand, competition)
